# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][covenant].

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a welcoming experience for
everyone, regardless of background or identity. We are committed to acting
and interacting in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive, diverse, and
healthy community.

## Standards

Contributors and maintainers are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully and in good faith
- Give and accept constructive feedback
- Take responsibility for mistakes and learn from them
- Prioritize what is best for the overall community

Behaviour that would make participation difficult for others is not
acceptable. For concrete examples, see the full Contributor Covenant
linked above.

## Enforcement

Issues can be reported confidentially by joining our
[Discord](https://discord.gg/88unzXDT) and DM'ing a maintainer with
`[CONDUCT]` in the first message.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion. Community
maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith
may face consequences determined by project leadership.
